Origin and intro

The SNU Tetris project is a variation of Tetris conceived on March 17th 2019, and after being abandoned for 11 months, was finally added to the SNU repository list for future work.

Tetris 128 is a variant of Tetris that is free and open source. It is 64x128 (128 bit, with 64 bit compatibility) software originally planned to be written in Python.

Features

* A graphics creator to create custom pieces with gradients, and patterns

* A major open-source cross-platform game of Tetris

* Play with unusual pieces, with Polyominos such as the Domino (2) Tromino (3) Tetromino (classic) (4) Pentomino (5) Hexomino (6) Heptomino (7) Octomino (8) Nonomino (9) and Decomino (10)

* Not tetris mode - a messy version of Tetris

* Music from all the Tetris games

* Graphics from 720p to 8K (future 16K and 32K support when needed)

* Printing mode - make art with Tetris pieces

* Play Tetris offline or through your web browser (online or offline)

* Coming soon: something related to Puyo Puyo

* The biggest graphical mode to Tetris 128 is the softbody mode, which gives pieces a softbody effect, and super high graphics. Examples of softbody Tetris are below, you can click here to go there now.
